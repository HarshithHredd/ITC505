Unscramble the Word

Addendum:

Welcome to "Unscramble the Word"! In this game, your objective is to decipher a word by rearranging its scrambled letters to form the correct word. Your challenge is to guess the correct word from the jumbled letters provided. Get ready to exercise your word-solving skills and enjoy the excitement of unscrambling words in this engaging game!

Difficulties Encountered and Solution:

Difficulties and Solution: The difficulties I encountered were while ensuring that user input was case-insensitive and free of unnecessary whitespace posed a challenge. This was addressed by converting the input to lowercase and utilizing the trim() method to eliminate leading and trailing whitespace, ensuring accurate comparison. Additionally, distinguishing between correct and incorrect guesses visually required careful handling. This was achieved by dynamically updating the message content and color based on the accuracy of the user's guess.
